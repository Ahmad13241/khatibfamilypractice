About Khatib Family Practice

Tailored Adult Medicine Serving Bullhead City, Laughlin, Fort Mohave & Mohave Valley Since 1998

What distinguishes Khatib Family Practice is our deliberate healthcare delivery model. Unlike high-volume clinics, Dr. Khatib maintains a smaller practice focused on fostering meaningful, long-term doctor-patient relationships. Many patients from throughout Bullhead City and surrounding communities like Laughlin and Fort Mohave have remained with us for years, benefiting from care that's both deeply personalized and clinically excellent, right here in the Tri-State area.
