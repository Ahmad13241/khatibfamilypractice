About Khatib Family Practice

Tailored Adult Medicine Since 1998

What distinguishes our approach is our healthcare delivery model. Unlike high-volume clinics serving dozens of patients daily, Dr. Khatib deliberately maintains a smaller practice to foster meaningful doctor-patient relationships. Many patients have remained with us for years—even decades—benefiting from care that's both deeply personalized and clinically excellent.
