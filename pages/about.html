Experience

Dr. Khatib has established himself as a trusted healthcare provider in the Bullhead City region, providing exceptional care to patients from Bullhead City, Laughlin, Fort Mohave, and Mohave Valley since 1998. His emphasis is on preventive medicine and individualized primary care for adult patients throughout the community.

Our Philosophy

Patient-centered care is the foundation of our practice throughout Bullhead City and surrounding communities

At Khatib Family Practice, we deliver thorough, attentive adult primary care to patients across Bullhead City, Laughlin, Fort Mohave, and Mohave Valley. Our approach centers on building enduring healthcare partnerships. Unlike high-volume clinics, our model ensures residents here receive genuinely tailored medicine and the focus required for effective care in the Tri-State area.

We believe exceptional healthcare requires time—time to listen, understand your unique situation, and develop the right approach. By maintaining a select patient roster, we create space for meaningful connections, leading to better health outcomes for our patients right here in the Bullhead City community and surrounding areas.

Our Practice

A welcoming environment designed for focused, attentive care

Comfortable Environment

Our cozy office setting creates a relaxed atmosphere where patients can discuss health concerns without feeling rushed or processed through a medical assembly line.

Thorough Understanding

Dr. Khatib invests time to develop a complete picture of your health history, family background, lifestyle factors, and goals—creating a foundation for more accurate diagnoses and effective treatment.

Continuity of Care

You'll see the same physician at every visit, eliminating the need to repeatedly explain your medical history. This consistency leads to better coordination, fewer medical errors, and more tailored care.

The Personalized Care Advantage

By design, we see fewer patients each day than typical practices. This enables us to offer longer appointments, minimal waiting, and a truly individualized approach. Staff members know you by name, and Dr. Khatib remains accessible for your healthcare needs.
