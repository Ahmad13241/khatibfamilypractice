New Patients (21+ Only)

For your first appointment, please bring:

  • Photo ID (must be 21 years or older)
  • Insurance card
  • Medical history
  • Current medications list
  • Previous medical records (if available)

Please arrive 15 minutes early to complete paperwork.

Download and complete these forms before your first visit:

  • Financial Policy
  • Records Request Form
  • New Patient Forms

Please print and complete these forms before your appointment, or arrive 15 minutes early to fill them out in our office.
