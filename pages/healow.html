Getting Started with healow

Follow these simple steps to set up your healow patient portal account

Setting up your healow account is easy and only takes a few minutes. Once set up, you'll have secure access to your health information anytime, anywhere.

  1. 1

    Download the healow App

    Get the free healow app from the App Store (iPhone) or Google Play (Android) on your mobile device.

  2. 2

    Create Your Account

    Enter your first name, last name, and date of birth to begin the account setup process.

  3. 3

    Find Our Practice

    Search for "Khatib Family Practice" or enter our practice code (DGAEAD) to connect with us.

  4. 4

    Set Up Security

    Create a secure PIN to protect your health information and ensure only you can access your records.

  5. 5

    Start Using healow

    Log in to access all your health information and begin using the portal's convenient features.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the healow Patient Portal

Does the healow app cost anything?

No, the healow app is completely free. We provide this service to our patients to help them access their health records and make better healthcare decisions.

Is my information secure in the healow app?

Yes, your information is completely secure. It is protected with a username and password/PIN number of your choice through SSL encryption.

How do I set up healow for multiple family members?

To add another family member to your healow app, you need to log out completely and then select "Begin Now" to add another account. You can then switch between accounts as needed.

What if I forget my password or PIN?

If you forget your password or PIN, you can use the "Forgot Password" option on the login screen. You'll receive instructions to reset your credentials via the email address associated with your account.

Can I access healow from my computer?

Yes, you can access the healow patient portal through any web browser by visiting healow.com. The web version offers the same features as the mobile app.
