New Patient Forms

Please complete these fillable PDF forms before your first appointment to save time during your visit. Dr. Khatib and our Bullhead City office provide medical care exclusively for adults age 21 and older, so all new patients must meet this age requirement.

Appointment Preparation

How to prepare for your first visit with Dr. Khatib:

  • Bring your completed forms (or arrive 15 minutes early to complete them)
  • Bring your insurance card and photo ID
  • Bring a list of current medications including dosages
  • Bring any relevant medical records or test results
  • Prepare a list of questions you may have for the doctor
