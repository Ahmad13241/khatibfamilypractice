About Dr. Nadim Khatib

Board Certified Family Medicine Physician serving the Tri-State area since 1998.

A Legacy of Care in Bullhead City

For over 25 years, Dr. Nadim Khatib has been a pillar of the Bullhead City medical community. Unlike transient providers in corporate clinics, Dr. Khatib has made this community his home. He understands the unique health challenges of our region and is dedicated to serving his neighbors with compassion and expertise.

His philosophy is simple: Treat every patient like family. This means listening without looking at the clock, explaining diagnoses in plain English, and advocating for your health above all else.

Our Philosophy

Why we choose to remain an independent private practice.

"We believe exceptional healthcare requires time—time to listen, understand your unique situation, and develop the right approach."

In an era where healthcare is increasingly dominated by large corporate entities, Khatib Family Practice stands apart. We are proudly independent. This means our loyalty lies solely with you, the patient—not with a hospital system, an insurance conglomerate, or corporate shareholders.

Dr. Khatib believes that exceptional healthcare requires the freedom to make decisions based purely on medical necessity and your personal values. By maintaining a private practice, we preserve the sanctity of the doctor-patient relationship, ensuring you receive unhurried, personalized attention that is becoming rare in modern medicine.
