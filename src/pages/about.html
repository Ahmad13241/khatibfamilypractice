About Dr. Nadim Khatib

A Legacy of Care in Bullhead City

Dr. Nadim Khatib has established himself as a trusted healthcare provider in the Bullhead City region, providing exceptional care to patients from Bullhead City, Laughlin, Fort Mohave, and Mohave Valley since 1998. His emphasis is on preventive medicine and individualized primary care for adult patients throughout the community.
