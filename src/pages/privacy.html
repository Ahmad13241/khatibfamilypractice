Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

At Khatib Family Practice, we are committed to protecting your privacy and the confidentiality of your medical information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, such as when you schedule an appointment, fill out forms, or communicate with us. This may include:

  • Name, address, and contact information
  • Date of birth and demographic details
  • Medical history and insurance information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide medical care and treatment
  • Process payments and insurance claims
  • Communicate with you about appointments and health reminders
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

3. HIPAA Compliance

We strictly adhere to the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). We do not share your protected health information (PHI) with third parties without your consent, except as required by law or for treatment, payment, and healthcare operations.

4. Website Security

Our website uses SSL encryption to protect data transmitted between your browser and our servers. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
