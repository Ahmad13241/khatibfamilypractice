Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring your photo ID, current insurance card, a list of current medications (or the bottles themselves), and any recent medical records or test results.

Do you offer same-day appointments?

Yes, we reserve time daily for urgent care needs. Please call as early as possible to secure a slot.

How do I get a prescription refill?

Please contact your pharmacy to request a refill. They will send us a request electronically. Allow 48-72 hours for processing.
