Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring your photo ID, current insurance card, a list of current medications (or the bottles themselves), and any recent medical records or test results from other providers.

Do you offer same-day appointments?

Yes, we reserve time daily for urgent care needs for our established patients. Please call as early as possible (starting at 8:00 AM) to secure a slot.

How do I get a prescription refill?

The fastest way is to contact your pharmacy to request a refill. They will send us a request electronically. Please allow 48-72 hours for processing, so don't wait until you are completely out of medication.

Do you treat children?

We specialize in adult medicine and primary care for patients aged 21 and older. We can refer you to excellent local pediatricians for younger family members.
